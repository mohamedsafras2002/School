About Zahira Ahadiyyah School

Zahira Ahadiyyah School, a leading educational institution in Dharga Town, blends Islamic values with modern education to empower young minds. With a commitment to academic excellence and a nurturing environment, we prepare students to lead with integrity and vision.

  • Comprehensive Islamic education alongside modern academic programs
  • State-of-the-art facilities promoting a balanced learning experience
  • A dedicated faculty focused on the holistic development of each student
  • Active participation in extracurricular activities like sports and cultural events
